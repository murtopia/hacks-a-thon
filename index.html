We're calling this a Hacks-a-Thon for a reason: none of us are pretending to be expert developers (except our expert developers of course), and it's okay to be a hack. The Seven2 Hacks-a-Thon is a focused, time-blocked experiment designed to help everyone on our team learn how to build with AI. All you need is an idea, curiosity, and a willingness to try.

01

The Plan

This playbook outlines the structure we'll follow to move from idea to live demo in just a few weeks. It's more than just a sprint. It's your chance to unlock a new creative superpower.

Outcomes

By the end of the Hacks-a-Thon, every participant will have:

  • Built a live prototype
  • Updated IdeaLab with their project URL
  • Demonstrated their work

Success is defined by participation, momentum, and growth — not perfection.

02

The Blocks

It's all about time blocking. Each block moves participants from idea to live demo while staying integrated into the normal rhythm of work.

ZERO

Kickoff

15 min

Introduce Loveable, explain vibe coding, set expectations, and outline the structure. Encourage the use of AI to help you work with AI.

Remove intimidation and create clarity before ideas begin.

Before

  • Calendar invite sent
  • Recording enabled
  • Loveable demo prepared
  • Account setup instructions ready
  • Helpful YouTube links ready
  • Slack channel (#hacks-a-thon) created
  • IdeaLab instructions shared

During

  • Record session
  • Share Loveable resources in Slack
  • Reinforce passion-driven projects

After

  • Share recording with Nick
  • Confirm subscription access for all

Next Day Check-In

Quick reactions and initial thoughts.

01

Sprint to the IdeaLab

30 min

Your chance to brainstorm, define and submit your project idea at IdeaLab.seven2.com

Commit to a direction and articulate why the idea matters to you.

Participant Requirements

  • Idea title
  • Who it's for
  • Problem it solves
  • Why it matters to you
  • Initial direction
  • Use AI to help you AI

After

  • 100% submission confirmed

Next Day Check-In

Share the title of your idea (teaser only).

02

Shark Tank, Minus the Sharks

45 min

Each participant delivers a 1-minute pitch with light feedback from the team.

Sharpen ideas and build collective energy.

Before

  • Pitch order is Status order
  • Timer ready with 50-second warning tone
  • Recording enabled

During

  • Enforce 1-minute limit
  • Keep feedback concise and encouraging

After

  • Encourage Slack follow-up
  • Participants refine IdeaLab entry if needed

Next Day Check-In

Did your idea change after getting feedback?

03

Documentation Is Everything

30 min

Develop specifics around your idea using AI, ZERO.Prmptr.ai, IdeaLab, or all three.

Translate the idea into a low-prompt, buildable direction.

Participant Requirements

  • Refined build direction
  • Defined first milestone
  • Updated IdeaLab entry

After

  • Confirm build direction exists

Next Day Check-In

What are you building first?

4A

Here We Go!

45 min

It's time to start your project in Loveable!

Jumpstart development of your idea.

Before

  • Confirm Loveable accounts are active
  • Office hours Zoom open

During

  • Encourage Slack progress posts
  • Provide live support

Participant Requirements

  • Begin building
  • Publish early working version (even rough)

Next Day Check-In

What did you get working?

4B

Build Session 2

45 min

Protected build time with optional office hours.

Iterate and improve.

During

  • Office hours open
  • Slack reminder to post progress

Participant Requirements

  • Improve functionality
  • Refine demo flow

Next Day Check-In

What improved?

4C

Your Final Build Session

45 min

This is your last time-blocked build session. However, you are free to work on it further in your free time.

Polish demo flow and prepare for showcase.

During

  • Office hours open
  • Encourage final polish

Participant Requirements

  • Final Loveable URL ready
  • IdeaLab updated with live link
  • 3-minute demo prepared

Next Day Check-In

Are you demo-ready?

FINAL

Showcase Showdown

2 × 60 min

Each participant presents a 3-minute (or less) demo followed by 2-minute Q&A from the team.

Celebrate learning, reflect on insights, and capture takeaways.

Before

  • Demo order finalized
  • Meeting link distributed
  • Recording enabled
  • All project URLs verified

During

  • Enforce time limits
  • Capture standout quotes
  • Ask reflection questions

Participant Requirements

  • 3-minute or less demo
  • Final link confirmed in IdeaLab
  • Summary form completed

06

Run Your Own

Want to run a Hacks-a-Thon at your company? Here's the framework.

A

Pick Your Platform

Choose an AI builder like Loveable. Give everyone a subscription. Lower the barrier to zero.

B

Block the Time

2–3 weeks. Short, focused sessions (15–45 min). Integrated into normal work. Use daily standups for check-ins.

C

Structure the Journey

Kickoff, ideation, pitches, documentation, build sessions, then a showcase. Each phase has a purpose.

D

Celebrate the Learning

Awards, reflections, demos. Success is participation and growth, not production-ready apps.
